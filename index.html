Flex & grid and their importance in Front-end development

The post image and text take 2 fractions of the whole width, while the right column takes 1 fraction, refers as grid-template-columns.

The grid-template-rows means the height by each element, the row-gap and the column-gap means the space between.

Grid functionality

  • grid-template-columns
  • grid-template-columns
  • row-gap
  • column-gap
  • grid-gap
  • grid-template-areas
